Who We Are

Built for reliability. Driven by innovation.

DesignOrbit Ltd is a UK-based technology and digital solutions company focused on delivering high-quality IT services, web development, and business infrastructure support.

Founded with a vision to help businesses scale efficiently in the digital age, DesignOrbit specialises in network administration, custom web application development, and cloud-based solutions including Google Workspace integration and support.

We work closely with startups and growing businesses to build reliable systems, improve operational efficiency, and create tailored digital products that meet modern business needs.
